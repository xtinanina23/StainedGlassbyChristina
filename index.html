What will your website include?

Graphics: The website will include high-quality images of my stained glass art. These images will allow visitors to see different pieces of my artwork.

Text: The website will provide information about my work, my creative process, and my experience. The homepage will have a brief introduction, and the Portfolio and Contact pages will contain more details about my art and how to reach me.

Links: There will be clickable links to navigate the site (e.g., Home, Portfolio, Contact). I’ll also include links to my social media profiles (like Instagram) and a contact form.

Audio and Video: Right now, I don’t plan on adding audio or video, but I might in the future. For example, I might add a video showing how I create my stained glass art.

Interactive Elements: The site will have a contact form, where users can send messages to me directly. The website will also work well on both desktop and mobile devices.

How will your website look?

Interface: The website will have a simple, clean design with a focus on the stained glass artwork. The layout will be easy to navigate, with clear headings and large images.

Color Scheme: The background will be neutral with dark text for readability. The site’s design will be minimalistic to highlight the artwork.

Typography: The font will be Bodoni or something similar, giving it a classy and professional feel. Headings will be bold and larger, while the body text will be easy to read.

Layout: The website will have a sticky header that stays at the top as you scroll. This will include links to the main pages (Home, Portfolio, Contact). The homepage will show a banner image of stained glass, with a short introduction about my work. The Portfolio page will show images of my stained glass art. The Contact page will include a contact form and my email address.
